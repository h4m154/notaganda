## About This Course

Conflict of Nations: World War 3 is a complex real-time strategy game that rewards careful planning, resource management, and diplomatic finesse. This comprehensive course will take you from your first game to confidently competing with experienced players.

## What You'll Learn

- **Game Fundamentals** - Understand the core mechanics that drive success in CoN
- **Early Game Strategy** - Set yourself up for victory from day one
- **Resource Management** - Maximize your economic output efficiently
- **Military Composition** - Build the right units for every situation
- **Diplomatic Skills** - Form alliances and navigate the political landscape
- **Advanced Tactics** - Master the strategies used by top players

## Who This Course Is For

This course is designed for:

- New players who want to learn the game properly from the start
- Intermediate players looking to break through to the next level
- Experienced players who want to refine their strategies

## Course Structure

The course is divided into 8 comprehensive lessons, each building on the previous one. We recommend going through them in order, but feel free to jump to specific topics if you're looking to improve in particular areas.
