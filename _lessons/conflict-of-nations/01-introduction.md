## Welcome to Conflict of Nations

Conflict of Nations: World War 3 is a free-to-play browser-based strategy game where you take control of a modern nation and compete against other players for global dominance. Games can last weeks or even months, making strategic planning essential.

## What Makes CoN Unique

Unlike many strategy games, Conflict of Nations operates in real-time. Your decisions have lasting consequences, and the persistent nature of the game means you need to plan for the long term.

### Key Features

- **Real-time gameplay** - Units move and build around the clock
- **Modern warfare** - Infantry, tanks, aircraft, naval vessels, and even nuclear weapons
- **Diplomacy** - Form alliances, trade, and negotiate with other players
- **Multiple victory conditions** - Win through conquest, points, or coalition dominance

## The Path to Victory

Success in Conflict of Nations requires mastering several interconnected systems:

1. **Economy** - Resource generation and management
2. **Military** - Building and commanding your armed forces
3. **Diplomacy** - Working with (and against) other players
4. **Strategy** - Long-term planning and adaptation

## What You'll Need

To succeed in this game, you'll need:

- **Patience** - This is a marathon, not a sprint
- **Strategic thinking** - Every decision matters
- **Time management** - Check in regularly, but you don't need to play 24/7
- **Adaptability** - Plans change; be ready to adjust

## Getting Started

In the next lesson, we'll cover the fundamental game mechanics you need to understand before diving into strategy. Make sure you have the game open and ready to follow along!

## Key Takeaways

- Conflict of Nations is a long-term strategy game
- Success requires balancing economy, military, and diplomacy
- Patience and planning are more important than constant activity
- Understanding the basics is essential before developing advanced strategies
